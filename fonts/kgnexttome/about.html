Le Petite Bistro is a quaint little restaurant that focuses on what's good: local food, pristine cooking, fair prices and an environment for families and friends. Our award winning restaurant has something for everyone. Our menu changes with the seasons to ensure quality fresh food for all customers who come to our little restaurant. We hold special events such as wedding dinners, birthday parties, family reunions and much more! There is no party too big for our restaurant staff to handle. We are dedicated to our customers, our food, and our service; we ensure that each person leaves with a fully tummy and a smile on their face.
